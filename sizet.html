Sizet

Sizet is a mobile application that allows user to search and view standard measurements for interior design

My Role

As a member of the Briebug software solutions team I served as the Ui/UX designer. I worked with a team of developers to see the app through development and insured its acceptance into the app store.

Process

Identifing the problem

I worked with the client to discover what the main problems they wanted to solve with Sizet were. Previously interior designers had carried around a large book of standard measurements, nicknamed “The Silver Bullet”. Because of the books size it was extremely difficult to find specs on the fly and was cumbersome to carry around.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Starting in sketch form I determined the organization of the app and the general layout. I then moved to wireframing where I used invision to prototype the app and conduct basic user testing. At this point I identified and existing problems and adjusted the apps design accordingly before Stylizing the interface.  

Icon Development

Since the Icons needed to portray each category was fairly complex I decided to create a custom icon pack for the application. Once the icons were complete I created an icon font to insure a light an easy implementation into the app.

Through Development

Once the designs were finalized I worked directly with a team of developers to see insure that my designs were implemented correctly, help solve any problems that came up along the way, and insure that they were excepted into the app store.
