I decided I wanted to pursue a career in Graphic Design when I was a sophomore in high school. Ever since then I have been on a high speed train towards reaching my goal. I recently gained my BFA in Art & Design with a concentration in Graphic Design from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo. In my time off from school I have had the opportunity to participate in two internships. One took me to my hometown of Half Moon Bay to work for a design firm called GraphicWorks. The other dropped me in the middle of the bustling city of Los Angeles to work for the marketing firm STARMEN. Both of these opportunities have allowed me to grow tremendously as a designer and have helped me to discover my passion for web design and branding.
